Our Mission

To democratize professional printing and design for businesses and individuals worldwide. We believe everyone deserves access to high-quality print products with an intuitive design experience — regardless of location, language, or technical skill.

What We Do
End-to-End Design & Print Solution
From free online design tools to global manufacturing and shipping, we handle the entire workflow so you can focus on your creative vision.

Free Design Editor

A powerful browser-based editor with templates, AI tools, background removal, and text effects. No software installation required.

Professional Printing

Honeycomb boards, fabric banners, acrylic products, business cards, stickers, packaging, and 500+ product types with premium quality.

Global Fulfillment

Manufactured in South Korea and shipped worldwide. Fast domestic delivery and international shipping to 190+ countries.

Eco-Friendly Materials

Honeycomb board displays, recyclable packaging, and sustainable printing practices. Reducing environmental impact without compromising quality.

Artist Marketplace

AI-Powered Support

Our Journey
Company Milestones
2024
Founded Chameleon Printing (Chameleon Canvas Inc.) in Hwaseong, South Korea. Launched domestic printing services with proprietary online design editor.
2025 Q1
Expanded to Japan (cafe0101.com) with full Japanese localization. Integrated Stripe payments for international transactions.
2025 Q2
Launched global platform (cafe3355.com) supporting English, Chinese, Arabic, Spanish, German, and French. Achieved 190+ country coverage.
2025 Q3
Introduced AI-powered design assistant, auto-quoting engine, and Artist Marketplace for user-generated print products.
2026
Launched chameleon.design as the unified global gateway with automatic geo-based language detection. Continued expansion of eco-friendly product lines.
